FAQ

Common customer questions.

These answers are written professionally but still simple.

Do you repair iPhones and Samsung phones?

Yes. iTop takes repair enquiries for iPhone and Samsung devices, including screens, batteries, charging ports, water damage checks and common faults.

Do you offer same-day repairs?

Same-day repair options are available for selected services, depending on the model, part availability and how busy the shop is.

Do you take online payment?

Not in this first version. The website is enquiry-based. iTop confirms the price, stock and repair time before any work starts.

Will my data be safe?

Customers should back up important data before repair where possible. iTop should not ask for unnecessary personal information, and customers should remove passcodes only when needed for testing.

Can I ask about unlocking?

Yes, but network unlocking is subject to device ownership, eligibility, availability and any required checks.

Do you supply accessories wholesale?

Yes. iTop takes wholesale enquiries for bulk accessories such as phone cases, screen protectors, chargers and cables.
