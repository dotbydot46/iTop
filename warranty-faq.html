Best website structure

General warranty page + warranty on each service.

This is the best approach for iTop. The general page explains the rules, and later each repair/product page shows the exact warranty for that item.

  • Repair pages can show warranty by repair type.
  • Future phone listings can show warranty per device.
  • Accessories can show stock, return or exchange notes separately.
  • Customers can still ask iTop to confirm everything on WhatsApp.

Important

Warranty should always be confirmed before payment or repair.

Because parts, models and device condition can be different, the website should avoid promising one fixed warranty for every item. The safer wording is: “Warranty confirmed before work starts.”

What warranty normally covers

Simple aftercare rules customers can understand.

Normally covered

  • Faults with the replaced part within the agreed warranty period
  • Repair workmanship issues linked to the original repair
  • Selected parts that fail under normal use
  • Warranty option confirmed on the receipt or message

Normally not covered

  • New cracks, drops, pressure marks or physical damage
  • Liquid damage, moisture damage or corrosion after repair
  • Misuse, tampering, third-party repair or opened devices
  • Software issues, data loss, account locks or network restrictions

FAQ

Common customer questions.

These answers are written carefully so the website stays professional and does not promise more than the shop can confirm.

Do all repairs get the same warranty?

No. Warranty depends on the repair type, part selected, device condition and availability. iTop confirms the exact warranty before the repair starts.

Can warranty be shown on each repair page?

Yes. That is the best plan. Each repair type can later show a small warranty badge such as “6 month warranty” or “12 month warranty available”.

Can refurbished phones have their own warranty?

Yes. When iTop starts listing phones, each phone can show its own warranty, condition, storage, battery health and what is included.

Do you offer same-day repairs?

Same-day options may be available for selected repairs when parts are in stock and enough time is available.

Do you take online payment?

Not in this version. Customers send an enquiry first, then iTop confirms price, stock, warranty and repair time directly.

Can you unlock phones?

Unlocking enquiries are subject to ownership, eligibility, network status and availability. iTop confirms this before accepting the enquiry.

Will my data be safe?

Customers should back up important data where possible. iTop should confirm any data-related risks before work begins.

Do accessories have warranty?

Accessory returns, exchange or warranty can be confirmed per product category later. For now, stock and product condition are confirmed directly by iTop.

Not sure which warranty applies?

Send the device model, repair needed or product name and iTop can confirm the correct option.
